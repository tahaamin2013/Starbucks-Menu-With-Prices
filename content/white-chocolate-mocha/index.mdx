## White Chocolate Mocha 
  
Starbucks White Chocolate Mocha is a supreme coffee drink; it always delights with its white chocolate flavour.

The standard recipe includes
- full-bodied Signature Espresso Roast,
- White chocolate mocha sauce and
- steamed hot 2% milk,
- It's then topped with a thick, frothy layer of milk foam and sweetened whipped cream.

The Signature Espresso Roast is bold, smooth, and caramel-flavoured. The white chocolate mocha sauce mixes sugar, condensed milk, coconut oil, cocoa butter, and more. The number of espresso shots and mocha sauce pumps varies depending on the drink size.

    | Standard Recipe                   | EspressoShots             | White Chocolate Mocha Sauce pumps   |
    |-----------------------------------|:-------------------------:|:-----------------------------------:| 
    | Short                             | 1                         | 2                                   |
    | Tall                              | 1                         | 3                                   |
    | Grande                            | 2                         | 4                                   |
    | Venti                             | 2                         | 3                                   |

If you like a smooth, vibrant, and slightly citrusy taste, you can customise your drink by choosing Blonde Espresso Roast.

If you don't like Signature or Blonde Espresso Roast, you can choose one of the following:

- ⅓ Decaf Espresso Roast
- ½ Decaf Espresso Roast
- ⅔ Decaf Espresso Roast
- Decaf Espresso Roast

You can also customise the number of Espresso shots for any size, up to a maximum of 12 shots.

Additionally, To customise the number of white chocolate mocha sauce pumps, they can range from zero to 12.

Starbucks uses steamed hot milk in its standard Cappuccino. The company also offers warm milk for those who prefer it, and they can make it extra hot upon request.

Regular whole milk typically contains 3.25% fat. Although Starbucks provides 2% fat milk in its standard Cappuccino, you may choose whole or non-fat milk.
Apart from the above milk options, various non-dairy milk alternatives are also available:

- almond,
- coconut,
- oat milk, and
- soy.

For those who prefer richer or alternative tastes, Starbucks also provides options like:
- heavy cream,
- Non-dairy vanilla sweet cream,
- vanilla sweet cream, and
- breve (half & half).

You can choose whether you prefer your drink without whipped cream, with a light touch, or with extra topping.
